# Ocado

**Company:** Ocado Group plc
**Country:** United Kingdom
**Industry:** Ecommerce / Online Grocery Technology
**ICP Score:** 9/10

## Why it fits our ICP

- World's largest dedicated online grocery retailer and grocery technology platform
- Operates both a retail business (Ocado.com) and technology platform (Ocado Smart Platform) licensed to retailers globally
- Extreme data complexity: millions of orders, personalisation at scale, perishable product management
- AI and machine learning are core to operations and product — explicitly technology-first culture
- Platform business creates B2B digital experience angle (retailer partners using Ocado Smart Platform)
- Listed on London Stock Exchange — significant revenue pressure to monetise technology platform
