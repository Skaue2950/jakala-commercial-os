## Strategic Context

Kingfisher's CTO manages a deliberate balance: centralised AI and technology platform (group level) vs. brand autonomy (B&Q, Screwfix, Castorama operate independently). This creates natural architecture complexity — multiple brands, markets, and data layers feeding into a shared AI infrastructure.

The speed of AI deployment (30+ initiatives, multiple brands, visual search, forecasting, personalisation) is outpacing data architecture governance. This is the commercial gap.
