# Boulanger

**Company:** Boulanger (Mulliez Group)
**Country:** France
**Industry:** Retail / Consumer Electronics & Appliances
**ICP Score:** 8/10

## Why it fits our ICP

- Leading French consumer electronics and home appliance retailer — 230+ stores + ecommerce
- Part of the Mulliez family group (also owns Leroy Merlin, Auchan, Decathlon) — group relationship potential
- Large product catalog: electronics, appliances, gaming, audio, photography
- Significant digital investment — customer experience and ecommerce are strategic priorities
- Services (delivery, installation, repair) integrated with retail creates rich digital experience opportunity
- AI and personalization growing as key competitive differentiators
