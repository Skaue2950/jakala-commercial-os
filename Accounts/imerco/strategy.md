# Imerco — Commercial Strategy

Last updated: 2026-03-08

---

## GTM Strategy: Data Revenue Unlock → Commerce Optimization

---

## Entry Offer

**Data Revenue Diagnostic — €50–80K**

Assess: product data completeness across 30,000+ SKUs, taxonomy quality (especially newly added brands and endless-aisles catalog), search relevance on imerco.dk, and personalization activation gap for 1.5M ImercoPlus loyalty members.

**Framing:** "You've built one of Denmark's strongest loyalty programs and grown your catalog by 28% in a year. The next growth lever is making that product data work harder — for search, discovery, and personalized recommendations."

**Price range:** €50–80K diagnostic → €200–500K Speedtrain + personalization program

---

## Why Now

- Catalog grew by 28% and 75 new brands + 7,000 SKUs added in 2023 alone — data quality is under pressure from rapid expansion
- ImercoPlus 1.5M members = loyalty data exists but personalization likely remains generic (RFM segments, not product-level)
- Endless aisles expansion = no physical shelf to "curate" — product data and search quality are the only merchandising tool
- IMPACT Commerce omnichannel case study shows digital ambition — they are investing in digital. The data layer is the gap.
- Automated pricing via Delfi — appetite for AI tooling confirmed
- Online revenue growing — ecommerce is increasingly material for the business

---

## Positioning

Position JAKALA as the **data and personalization layer on top of their IMPACT Commerce + CDP investment** — not a platform replacement, not a loyalty platform.

Key messages:
1. "Your 1.5M loyalty members and CDP give you the customer data. The gap is product data quality — without it, personalization is generic and search misses intent."
2. "You've added 75 brands and 7,000 SKUs in one year. At that pace, taxonomy consistency and data completeness don't scale automatically."
3. "A 6-week diagnostic quantifies the revenue opportunity in your existing catalog — we've seen 5–15% conversion uplift from fixing the data layer for retailers your size."

Do NOT:
- Position against IMPACT Commerce (existing partner — don't threaten their relationship)
- Lead with Shopify or platform change language
- Use generic "digital transformation" framing
