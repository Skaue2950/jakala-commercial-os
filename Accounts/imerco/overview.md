## Key Signals

- **30,000+ SKUs and growing rapidly** — catalog complexity is increasing. Product data quality and taxonomy are under pressure.
- **ImercoPlus 1.5M members** — loyalty data is rich but personalization activation is likely generic given scale.
- **Endless aisles expanding** — adding brands fast means PIM and product data governance is critical.
- **IMPACT Commerce partnership active** — implementation partner in place, but data layer above it is an open gap.
- **AI pricing implemented** — appetite for AI tooling is clear. Data readiness for broader AI is the question.
- **42% ecommerce revenue growth potential** — omnichannel push means digital revenue will grow, raising stakes on product discovery quality.
