## Company Overview

Simployer is a Norwegian HR technology company providing cloud-based HR management software to SME and mid-market companies across the Nordics. The platform covers HR administration, compliance, employee handbooks, performance management, and engagement. Backed by Ferd (Norwegian family office). In October 2024, Simployer acquired AlexisHR (Sweden), significantly expanding its Swedish footprint.
