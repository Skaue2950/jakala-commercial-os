## Company Overview

Siteimprove is a Danish SaaS company founded in 2003, providing an AI-powered digital content intelligence platform. The platform unifies web accessibility compliance (WCAG), analytics, SEO/AEO, and content strategy into a single workspace. Used by enterprise, government, and education institutions globally. Majority-owned by Nordic Capital (PE). Recognized as a Leader in Forrester Wave: Digital Accessibility Platforms, Q4 2025.
