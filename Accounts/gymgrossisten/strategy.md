## GTM Strategy: Data Revenue Unlock → Commerce Optimization

---

## Entry Offer

**Data Revenue Diagnostic — €50–80K**

Assess: product data completeness across 5 ecommerce sites (Gymgrossisten, Bodystore, Proteinfabrikken, Bodyman, Fitness Market), catalog taxonomy consistency across markets (SE, NO, DK), search relevance and zero-results rate, and regulatory attribute completeness (supplements require nutrition facts, allergens, certifications).

**Framing:** "You've delivered a strong financial turnaround in 2024. The next growth lever is making your 5 Nordic ecommerce sites convert better. At SEK 1.23B across multi-market sites with complex supplement catalogs, product data quality is likely the silent conversion gap."

**Price range:** €50–80K diagnostic → €200–400K Speedtrain multi-site orchestration

---

## Why Now

- Financial turnaround completed (SEK -15M to +27M in 2024) — the business is now in growth mode. Buying window is open.
- 5 separate ecommerce sites = fragmented catalog data. Each site likely has inconsistent taxonomy, attribute quality, and search performance.
- Supplement catalog = regulatory-intensive attributes (allergens, nutrition facts, certifications, country-specific compliance). Data quality errors create legal and conversion risk.
- 24M site visits/year = significant traffic but thin margins historically. Conversion rate improvement is highest-leverage growth driver.
- Orkla digital commerce investment signals group-level support for digital growth
- Post-turnaround CFO/CEO will be receptive to ROI-framed commercial investments

## Positioning

Position JAKALA as the **revenue optimizer for multi-site Nordic ecommerce** — not a platform vendor or generic agency.

Key messages:
1. "You have 5 Nordic ecommerce sites serving a shared catalog of supplements and activewear. Multi-site means multi-fragmentation: different taxonomy, different attribute quality, different search configurations."
2. "Supplement catalogs are the hardest product data to get right — regulatory attributes, certification fields, allergen declarations. A diagnostic tells you exactly where the gaps are and what they're costing in conversion."
3. "Post-turnaround, you need growth without a major capex. A diagnostic at €50–80K gives you a prioritised fix roadmap with clear revenue hypotheses."

Do NOT:
- Lead with platform replacement
- Ignore the Orkla parent context — any engagement needs to align with group digital strategy
- Overpromise — the CFO will scrutinize the ROI case

## Competitive Considerations

- **Orkla group:** Parent company context means decisions may go up to group level. Always check if there's a group digital governance process.
- **Existing platform vendors:** 5 sites may run on different stacks — diagnostic reveals this. Position as the unifying intelligence layer, not a new platform.
- **Regulatory complexity:** Supplement data has EU/Nordic compliance requirements — JAKALA's data diagnostic can include compliance attribute audit. Differentiator.
