## Digital & Tech Snapshot

HSNG operates multiple ecommerce portals across the Nordic market, covering Sweden, Norway, and Denmark under different brand identities. Each portal caters to local markets with localized product ranges. The multi-brand, multi-market setup creates significant catalog and product data management complexity. The company went from a loss of SEK -15M in 2023 to a profit of SEK 27M in 2024 — a turnaround that will pressure further commercial performance investment.

## Key Signals

- **SEK 1.23B revenue across 5 ecommerce sites** — this is a significant Nordic ecommerce operation.
- **Turnaround complete 2024** — profitability restored, now positioned to invest in growth. This is a buying window.
- **5 separate ecommerce sites** — multi-brand, multi-market catalog management is almost certainly fragmented. PIM opportunity.
- **24M site visits/year** — high traffic, but profitability was thin. Search quality and product discovery directly impact conversion.
- **Supplements + activewear catalog** — complex attribute requirements (nutrition facts, certifications, allergens, materials, sizes). Low data quality = high return rates.
- **Orkla parent** — Orkla has a stated digital commerce strategy. HSNG is the vanguard. Group buy-in could accelerate deals.
- **DK and NO sites alongside SE** — multi-market = multi-language taxonomy, currency, regulatory attributes.
