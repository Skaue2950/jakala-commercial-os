# Coop Norge

**Company:** Coop Norge
**Country:** Norway
**Industry:** Retail / Grocery
**ICP Score:** 7/10

## Why it fits our ICP

- One of Norway's two largest grocery retail groups (competing with NorgesGruppen)
- Multi-format retail: hypermarkets, supermarkets, convenience, online
- Member-owned cooperative structure creates complexity in data governance and digital investment
- Growing ecommerce ambitions and loyalty/personalization programs
- Large product catalog across own-label and branded products
- Digital transformation ongoing — IT modernization and ecommerce platform upgrades create entry points
