# NorgesGruppen

**Company:** NorgesGruppen ASA
**Country:** Norway
**Industry:** Retail / Grocery
**ICP Score:** 7/10

## Why it fits our ICP

- Norway's largest grocery retail group — brands include KIWI, Meny, Spar, Joker
- Controls approximately 43% of Norwegian grocery market
- Significant digital ambitions through Oda partnership (divested) and own online channels
- Loyalty program (Trumf) creates rich customer data — personalization opportunity
- Multi-brand, multi-format complexity creates data and experience fragmentation
- Digital transformation of in-store and online experience is ongoing
