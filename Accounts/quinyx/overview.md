## Company Overview

Quinyx is a Swedish workforce management SaaS company founded in 2005 by Erik Fjellborg. It provides AI-driven scheduling, demand forecasting, labor optimization, time reporting, task management, and employee communication tools. The platform is recognized by IDC as a Leader in the 2025–2026 MarketScape for EMEA AI-Enabled Workforce Management. Primary markets include Nordics, UK, Germany, and US.
