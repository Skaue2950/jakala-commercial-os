## Company Overview

Heimdal Security is a Danish cybersecurity SaaS company founded in 2014. It provides a unified cybersecurity platform covering next-gen antivirus, DNS filtering, email security, privileged access management (PAM), and endpoint detection and response. The platform serves 15,000+ customers across 45 countries.
