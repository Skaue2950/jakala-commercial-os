## GTM Strategy: AI Readiness Accelerator → Data Revenue Unlock

---

## Entry Offer

**AI Readiness Diagnostic — €75–100K**

Assess: AI and data architecture readiness across Bestseller's multi-brand ecommerce ecosystem. Focus on: PIM fragmentation across 13+ brands, product data consistency across D2C + wholesale + marketplace channels, and readiness to power Voyado loyalty personalization with product-level data.

**Framing:** "Your Voyado partnership shows you're serious about customer data and retention. The next question is whether your product data is good enough to power the personalized recommendations Voyado promises. Across 13 brands, 70 markets, and three channels, that's rarely consistent."

**Price range:** €75–100K diagnostic → €400–800K multi-brand data program → Speedtrain rollout

---

## Why Now

- Voyado partnership is recent (2024/25) — they are in the early adoption phase. This creates a natural "make the investment work" conversation.
- 13+ brands, 70+ markets = product data fragmentation is almost certain. No confirmed central PIM.
- MobiLoud mobile apps for ONLY and JACK & JONES = digital investment is happening. Data layer is the gap.
- 38B DKK revenue (+7% YoY) — scale means even small data quality improvements = material revenue
- D2C growing vs. wholesale dependency — quality of product data on own sites becomes increasingly revenue-critical
- Family-owned long-term horizon — they invest when the case is clear, not short-term reactive

---

## Positioning

Position JAKALA as the **data intelligence layer that makes their Voyado investment perform** — not a platform vendor, not competing with any existing partner.

Key messages:
1. "Voyado works on customer segmentation. But personalized product recommendations require the product data to be complete, consistent, and correctly attributed. Across 13 brands and 70 markets, that's a significant data challenge."
2. "Most multi-brand groups discover the same thing: each brand has evolved its own product data structure. The catalog is fragmented — and that shows up in search, discovery, and personalization quality."
3. "A 6-week diagnostic across 2 of your largest brands (JACK & JONES + ONLY) gives you a quantified view of the gap and a clear roadmap to fix it."

Do NOT:
- Challenge their existing Voyado partnership
- Lead with platform or ecommerce build conversation
- Use generic "AI transformation" — they are commercially sophisticated and will see through it

## Competitive Considerations

- **Voyado:** Active CRM partner — do not compete. JAKALA is the product data layer that makes Voyado work.
- **MobiLoud:** App implementation partner — irrelevant to JAKALA's entry.
- **Scale:** Bestseller operates at €5B+ scale. A diagnostic entry at €75–100K is proportionally tiny. Frame this as a risk-free investment to validate the opportunity.
- **Internal tech team:** At this scale they have internal teams. Position as specialist augmentation with proven multi-brand credentials.
