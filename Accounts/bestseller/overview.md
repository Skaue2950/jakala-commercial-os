## Digital & Tech Snapshot

Bestseller is a massive multi-brand fashion group operating individual ecommerce sites across 13+ brands in 70+ markets. They use mobile apps built on MobiLoud for brands like ONLY and JACK & JONES. They recently partnered with Voyado for loyalty/CRM activation, signaling investment in customer data and retention. Their ecommerce is brand-led and market-led — creating significant product data fragmentation across the group.

## Key Signals

- **13+ brands, 70+ markets** — product data is almost certainly fragmented per brand, per market. This is a textbook Speedtrain / Data Revenue Diagnostic opportunity.
- **Voyado CRM partnership** — appetite for customer data activation confirmed. Next layer: product data quality to power personalization.
- **38B DKK revenue** — scale means even 1% improvement in product discovery = material revenue impact.
- **Family-owned** — long-term investment horizon. Not driven by quarterly earnings pressure.
- **D2C brand sites + wholesale + marketplaces** — triple-channel complexity. Catalog consistency across channels is a real problem.
- **No confirmed PIM** — multi-brand, multi-market without confirmed central PIM = almost certain data quality opportunity.
- **MobiLoud apps** — mobile-first ecommerce signals commercial sophistication but may also signal technical debt in backend systems.
