**Company:** Europris ASA
**Country:** Norway
**Industry:** Retail / Discount

- Growing ecommerce investment with ambitions to improve digital channel
- Very high SKU count across diverse categories (household, garden, toys, food)
- Product data management is a known challenge in discount retail
- Opportunity to help structure product data and improve digital discovery
