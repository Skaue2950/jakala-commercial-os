## Digital & Tech Snapshot

Plantagen Sverige is the Swedish arm of Plantasjen Group (Norway HQ). The group-wide Crystallize headless commerce + PIM + DAM platform went live in early 2025, covering Norway, Sweden, and Finland. Sweden shares the same Crystallize implementation as Norway, with multi-language and multi-market support. The Swedish operation is a significant retailer in its own right and increasingly drives its own digital agenda within the group.

## Key Signals

- **Crystallize live Q1 2025** — 12 months into adoption. Activation layer needed: search quality, personalization, catalog taxonomy.
- **PE-owned (Apax)** — EBITDA pressure = ROI-focused buying. Entry offers must have a clear payback case.
- **34 stores in Sweden** — large physical network means online plays a supporting role, but digital investment is growing.
- **Shared platform with Norway** — a deal with Plantagen Sverige (SE) could expand to Plantasjen (NO) group-wide. Strategic leverage.
- **Seasonal catalog** — high seasonality means product data quality determines search relevance during peak periods (spring rush = revenue-critical).
- **AI enrichment in Crystallize is basic** — advanced catalog scoring, taxonomy quality, and search optimization is the next layer.
- **CIO Olav Fyldeng** — group CIO (Norway-based) is the technology decision-maker for both NO and SE. One buyer for two markets.
