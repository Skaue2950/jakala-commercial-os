# Decathlon

**Company:** Decathlon SE
**Country:** France
**Industry:** Retail / Sports Consumer Brand
**ICP Score:** 9/10

## Why it fits our ICP

- World's largest sports retailer — 1,700+ stores in 60+ countries + major ecommerce
- Vertically integrated: designs, manufactures and sells its own brands (Quechua, B'Twin, etc.)
- Enormous product catalog across 70+ sports disciplines — extreme complexity
- Explicit digital-first strategy: major investment in ecommerce, data, and product experience
- Own-brand product data management is a strategic capability — PIM/MDM at scale
- AI and personalization are key to sport-specific discovery and recommendation
- B2B opportunity: clubs, schools, corporate sports programs as customer segment
