## Company Overview

Cognite is a Norwegian industrial AI and data platform company. Its flagship product, Cognite Data Fusion (CDF), contextualizes OT/IT data at scale, enabling AI-driven operations for heavy-asset industries. The platform is deployed by major operators including Equinor, Aker BP, and Siemens.
