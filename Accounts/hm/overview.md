# H&M

**Company:** H & M Hennes & Mauritz AB
**Country:** Sweden
**Industry:** Retail / Fashion Consumer Brand
**ICP Score:** 9/10

## Why it fits our ICP

- One of the world's largest fashion retailers — operates in 70+ markets
- Enormous product catalog with rapid seasonal turnover across multiple brands (H&M, COS, & Other Stories, Arket, Monki, Weekday)
- Digital transformation is a core strategic priority — major investment in ecommerce, data, and AI
- Complex multi-brand, multi-market architecture creates significant data and experience fragmentation
- Publicly stated ambitions around AI personalization and data-driven merchandising
- Ecommerce revenue is material and growing — performance improvements directly impact shareholder value
