## Company Overview

Oneflow is a Swedish publicly listed (Nasdaq First North) contract lifecycle management platform. It transforms static contracts into dynamic, data-driven assets — covering creation, negotiation, signing, and renewal. The platform serves enterprise and mid-market customers across 45+ countries, with 41% of revenue now generated outside Sweden.
