## Digital & Tech Snapshot

Boozt runs a fully proprietary, own-built ecommerce platform — built in-house since 2010 and scaled to 200+ developers. No off-the-shelf ecommerce platform. All systems, infrastructure, R&D, and the ecommerce platform are owned internally.
