## Digital & Tech Snapshot

Lyko runs a custom ecommerce platform built with Avensia (Avensia SCOPE headless layer, Optimizely/Episerver back-end). The platform has been live since ~2018 and powered Lyko's expansion across 8 markets. Lyko is a pure-play digital-first beauty retailer with manufacturing, warehouses, and 3PL in-house.

## Key Signals

- **60,000+ SKUs across 1,000+ brands** — enormous catalog complexity. Product data quality and taxonomy are likely inconsistent across brands, languages, and markets.
- **8-market expansion ongoing** — multi-language, multi-market catalog management is a known scaling pain point.
- **Warehouse automation live 2025** — operational investment is ahead of digital product discovery investment. Imbalance = opportunity.
- **Own brands growing** — own-brand product data (fewer external data feeds) = higher internal data governance requirement.
- **Avensia platform is mature** — potential friction between growth ambitions and current platform's data layer capabilities.
- **Revenue 750M SEK, growing above industry average** — financial capacity for investment exists.
