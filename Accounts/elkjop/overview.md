# Elkjøp

**Company:** Elkjøp Nordic
**Country:** Norway
**Industry:** Retail / Consumer Electronics
**ICP Score:** 8/10

## Why it fits our ICP

- Largest consumer electronics retailer in the Nordics with massive product catalog (100k+ SKUs)
- Complex omnichannel setup: physical stores + ecommerce across 6 markets
- Product data fragmentation across vendors, categories and markets is a known challenge
- Digital revenue growing but search and discovery performance likely under-optimized
- Part of Currys plc group — decisions influenced at group level but Nordic operations are semi-autonomous
- Strong candidate for Data Revenue Unlock and Commerce Optimization entry
