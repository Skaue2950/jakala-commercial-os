## GTM Strategy: Commerce Optimization → Data Revenue Unlock

---

## Entry Offer

**Commerce Optimization Pilot — €40–70K**

Focused 8-week pilot on product discovery and search relevance on the new Crystallize platform. Assess: taxonomy quality for seasonal garden catalog, search relevance across categories, catalog attribute completeness, and multi-market consistency (NO/SE/FI).

**Framing:** "You've done the hard work of migrating to Crystallize. The next question is: is your catalog data good enough to make search and discovery work as well as your new platform can? A focused 8-week pilot shows you exactly what the catalog gaps are and what they're costing."

**Price range:** €40–70K pilot → €200–400K Data Revenue Diagnostic + Speedtrain program

---

## Why Now

- Crystallize PIM/DAM went live Q1 2025 — they are now 12 months in. Classic "post-PIM adoption" phase — time for activation layer
- CIO Olav Fyldeng is on record in the Crystallize case study — he is publicly visible and named
- PE-owned by Apax — performance pressure means ROI-framed commercial conversations are welcome
- Seasonal catalog = revenue is concentrated in spring/summer peaks. Poor search quality during peak = material revenue loss
- Multi-market (NO/SE/FI) on a single platform — taxonomy consistency across markets is almost certainly imperfect
- Crystallize's AI enrichment is product-level automation, not catalog intelligence or search optimization

---

## Positioning

Position JAKALA as the **commerce intelligence layer on top of Crystallize** — not competing with Crystallize, adding the next capability.

Key messages:
1. "Crystallize is the right platform. The next layer is making your catalog data perform — taxonomy consistency, attribute completeness, and search relevance during your spring peak."
2. "PE-owned retailers need to see a clear payback. We scope the pilot to show a measurable impact on search conversion within 8 weeks."
3. "Your garden catalog is seasonal — the revenue window is narrow. We make sure your platform's search and discovery performs at peak."

Do NOT:
- Position against Crystallize (recent investment — don't threaten it)
- Lead with DXP or platform replacement
- Overpromise — PE owners are commercial and analytical
