## Digital & Tech Snapshot

Plantasjen completed a major digital transformation in Q1 2025, adopting Crystallize (Norwegian headless commerce / PIM platform) to replace legacy systems. The project unified PIM, DAM, workflow management, and multi-language/multi-market content management. AI-powered product enrichment automation was part of the implementation. Go-live achieved within 3 months.

## Key Signals

- **Crystallize PIM/DAM went live Q1 2025** — they are now 12 months into adoption. Classic "PIM deployed, activation layer needed" opportunity.
- **CIO Olav Fyldeng quoted in Crystallize case study** — he is the technical buyer, named and on record.
- **PE-owned by Apax** — PE ownership = performance pressure = urgency for digital revenue improvement.
- **Seasonal catalog complexity** — plants, garden furniture, outdoor accessories = high variation, attribute-heavy catalog. AI scoring and catalog quality are natural next steps.
- **3-market rollout** — multi-language taxonomy consistency is likely uneven across NO/SE/FI markets.
- **Crystallize is a PIM/DAM, not a commerce optimization platform** — the search, discovery, and merchandising layer is the gap JAKALA fills.
