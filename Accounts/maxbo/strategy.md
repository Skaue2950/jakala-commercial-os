# MAXBO — GTM Strategy

## GTM Strategy Fit

**Primary: Strategy 1 — Data Revenue Unlock**
**Secondary: Strategy 3 — Commerce Optimization** (if entry is via ecommerce/commercial rather than tech)

---

## Why Data Revenue Unlock

| Signal | Status |
|---|---|
| Large, complex product catalog (hundreds of thousands of SKUs) | ✅ |
| Product data fragmented across categories, suppliers, ERP | ✅ |
| Search and product discovery are a known retail weak spot | ✅ |
| Revenue pressure on digital channel performance | ✅ |
| Technical/architecture buyer exists | ✅ |

MAXBO is a large Norwegian home improvement and building materials retailer. As a physical-first business expanding digitally, the core problem is product data quality and completeness — which directly limits search relevance, online discovery, and conversion.

---

## Entry Offer

**Data Revenue Diagnostic**

Opening frame: *"You have a large catalog and growing digital ambitions. Let us show you where broken or incomplete product data is costing you revenue."*

## Primary Promise

Unlock hidden revenue by fixing the product data foundation.
