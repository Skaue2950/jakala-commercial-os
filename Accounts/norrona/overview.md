## Digital & Tech Snapshot

Norrona is a family-owned premium outdoor brand with a direct-to-consumer ecommerce operation. They launched a major new Oslo headquarters and flagship store recently, and are actively expanding in the US (Boulder, CO office). Their ecommerce platform history includes EPiServer (noted 2014 implementation). Current stack details are not publicly confirmed, but the brand has grown its D2C channel significantly. Revenue per market and channel split indicates the digital channel is growing but still a secondary channel to wholesale and flagship stores.

## Key Signals

- **D2C revenue $19M online** — significant but below brand potential. D2C acceleration = platform and discovery investment needed.
- **US expansion active** — multi-market ecommerce complexity (currencies, sizes, attributes) will grow.
- **Family-owned, 4th generation** — long-term thinking, brand quality focus. Not chasing trends.
- **Premium technical products** — complex attribute sets (technical specs, certifications, materials, fit). Poor product data = lost conversions.
- **No public platform signal** — potential Magento/Adobe Commerce or outdated EPiServer stack. Replatform or Shopify Check-up is the entry.
- **Strict no-discount policy** — conversion depends entirely on product presentation, search quality, and discovery — not promotions.
- **Upcycled product lines** — new product types require new taxonomy and data workflows.
