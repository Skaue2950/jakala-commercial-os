**Company:** Oda (formerly Kolonial.no)
**Country:** Norway
**Industry:** Ecommerce / Online Grocery

- Product catalog complexity: fresh, frozen, ambient, own-label across thousands of SKUs
- Personalization and recommendations are critical to basket size and retention
- Tech-forward culture — raised significant venture capital, invested in logistics and data tech
- AI/ML already embedded in operations (demand forecasting, routing) — expansion to experience layer is natural
- Direct digital revenue dependency makes performance improvements high priority
