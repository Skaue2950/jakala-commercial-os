## Key Discovery Signals

### PIM/Platform Post-Activation Gap
- Plantasjen / Plantagen Sverige — Crystallize PIM live Q1 2025. 12 months in = activation layer needed.
- Polarn O. Pyret — Optimizely DXP mature. Circular commerce (PO.P Second Hand) creating new data complexity.

### Multi-Market / Multi-Brand Fragmentation
- Bestseller — 13 brands, 70 markets, no confirmed central PIM. Voyado CRM partnership signals data appetite.
- Lyko — 60,000 SKUs from 1,000 brands across 8 markets. Multi-language taxonomy challenge.
- Gymgrossisten — 5 ecommerce sites (SE, NO, DK). Supplement catalog with regulatory attributes.

### Loyalty Data Underutilised
- Imerco — 1.5M ImercoPlus members. CDP active. Product data limits personalization activation.

### D2C Brand on Likely Legacy Platform
- Norrona — Premium outdoor D2C, US expansion, platform likely aging since 2014 EPiServer implementation.

### Financial Event (PE or Turnaround)
- Gymgrossisten — SEK -15M to +27M turnaround 2024. Growth investment window open.
- Plantasjen / Plantagen Sverige — Apax PE. EBITDA pressure = ROI-framed buying preferred.
