What it does

Every commercial function, automated

From lead discovery to pipeline forecasting — the OS handles the heavy lifting.

Automated Lead Discovery
Tavily scans Nordic news and signals daily. Claude scores each company against ICP criteria. Qualifying leads land directly in your pipeline.
Service Enrichment
Every account is automatically matched to the most relevant JAKALA services. Activation records are created and updated as new signals emerge.
Outreach Generation
Generate hyper-personalised outreach for any account and buyer in seconds. Calibrated to GTM strategy, tech stack and timing signals.
Meeting Intelligence
Paste meeting notes. The OS extracts key insights, updates account files, rewrites next actions and identifies the next commercial move.
Pipeline Forecasting
Probability-weighted board forecasts. Base case, upside and stretch scenarios. Updated automatically as deal stages change.
Deck Generation
One click generates a tailored 5-slide discovery deck per account — ready to download as PPTX and customise before the meeting.
