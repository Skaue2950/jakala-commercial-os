# Lead Radar

Act as a market intelligence analyst.

Search for relevant signals in:

- Norway
- Sweden
- Denmark

**Geographic scope is strictly Nordic — DK, NO, SE only. Ignore signals from UK, France, and all other markets.**

Focus on industries defined in intelligence/market-watch.md.

Identify signals such as:

- AI initiatives
- ecommerce platform changes
- leadership hires
- digital transformation programs
- new digital strategies

For each signal:

1. identify the company
2. explain why it matters
3. match to GTM strategy
4. suggest entry offer
5. estimate deal potential

Log promising leads in intelligence/lead-log.md.
