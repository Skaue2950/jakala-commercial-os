# Deal Priorities

Act as a CRO reviewing the current commercial situation.

Analyze:

- pipeline/active.md
- accounts/*
- signals/log.md
- knowledge/gtm-strategy.md
- knowledge/deal-scoring.md
- knowledge/icp-scoring.md
- knowledge/strategy-mapping.md

Tasks:

1. Rank the top opportunities.
2. Score each opportunity using the deal scoring model.
3. Identify the best GTM strategy for each opportunity.
4. Identify the correct entry offer.
5. Recommend the next commercial action.

Output format:

## Top Priorities

List the top 3 opportunities to focus on now.

For each opportunity include:

- company
- deal score
- ICP score
- recommended GTM strategy
- entry offer
- next action

## Emerging Opportunities

Signals that could become future deals.

## Risks

Deals that may stall or lack expansion potential.

## Recommended Focus This Week

Concrete actions to move the most important deals forward.
