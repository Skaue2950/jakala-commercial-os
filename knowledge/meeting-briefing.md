# Meeting Briefing Framework

Use this framework to prepare for sales meetings.

Goal:

Understand the company, identify the core problem, and position the correct entry diagnostic.

---

# Company Snapshot

Identify:

- industry
- size
- digital maturity
- ecommerce or digital importance

---

# Digital Ecosystem

Assess:

- ecommerce platforms
- CMS / DXP
- APIs and integrations
- data architecture

Look for signals such as:

- fragmented systems
- slow digital delivery
- legacy architecture

---

# Strategic Problem

Determine which problem from the problem-library best fits:

- Product data chaos
- AI ambition without data
- Digital revenue leakage
- Fragmented digital experience

Explain why this problem is likely relevant.

---

# GTM Strategy

Identify:

Best GTM strategy from:

- Data Revenue Unlock
- AI Readiness Accelerator
- Commerce Optimization
- Experience Transformation

---

# Entry Offer

Select one diagnostic:

- Data Revenue Diagnostic
- AI Readiness Diagnostic
- Commerce Optimization Pilot
- Executive Experience Strategy

Explain why this is the best entry point.

---

# Buyer Map

Identify likely stakeholders:

Technical buyers:
- CTO
- CIO
- Digital Architect

Commercial buyers:
- CMO
- Ecommerce Director

Strategic buyers:
- Transformation lead
- Head of Digital

---

# Meeting Narrative

Structure:

1. Discovery
2. Insight
3. Diagnostic proposal

---
