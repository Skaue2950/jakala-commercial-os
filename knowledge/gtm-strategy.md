# GTM Strategy v2

## Strategic Thesis

Selling large digital transformation programs directly is increasingly difficult.

Clients rarely start with:
- DXP platform migration
- large transformation programs

Instead, they start with **smaller problem-driven initiatives**.

### Proposed GTM Model

Use a **diagnostic-led go-to-market approach** that:

1. Opens client conversations with urgent business problems
2. Creates small entry engagements
3. Expands into larger transformation programs

The goal is **not to sell DXP first**.

The goal is to create a **repeatable entry motion that leads to transformation work.**
