# ICP Scoring Model

This file evaluates whether a company matches our Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).

Score companies from 1–10.

## 1. Industry Fit (1–2 points)

High score if company is:

- retail
- high-SKU ecommerce
- consumer brand
- B2B distributor with large product catalog

## 2. Data Complexity (1–2 points)

High score if:

- large product catalog
- fragmented systems
- inconsistent product data
- multiple integrations

## 3. AI Potential (1–2 points)

High score if:

- exploring AI initiatives
- wants personalization
- needs better data infrastructure
- leadership discussing AI transformation

## 4. Architecture Complexity (1–2 points)

High score if:

- multiple platforms
- legacy systems
- slow APIs
- composable architecture discussions

## 5. Revenue Impact (1–2 points)

High score if:

- digital channel drives significant revenue
- ecommerce performance matters
- product discovery directly impacts sales

## Score Interpretation

9–10 = Ideal ICP  
7–8 = Strong fit  
5–6 = Possible opportunity  
1–4 = Weak ICP fit

## Rule

Always determine:

1. ICP score
2. Best GTM strategy
3. Best entry offer
4. Next action
