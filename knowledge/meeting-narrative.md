# First Meeting Narrative

Meeting length: 30–45 minutes.

Goal:

Identify the business problem and propose a diagnostic.

---

# Meeting Structure

## Part 1 — Discovery

Questions:

Where does your product data live today?

Are you currently exploring AI initiatives?

What challenges do you face connecting systems?

---

## Part 2 — Insight

Explain:

Most organizations already have the tools they need.

What they lack is structured and accessible data.

---

## Part 3 — Diagnostic Proposal

Propose one of:

- Data Revenue Diagnostic
- AI Readiness Diagnostic
