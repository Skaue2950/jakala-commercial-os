# Outreach Playbook

This file defines how we approach outbound conversations.

Goal:
Open a conversation around a real business problem that leads to a diagnostic engagement.

Avoid:
Selling platforms or large transformation programs in the first contact.

Always lead with:
A problem that the buyer already recognizes.

---

# Outreach Structure

Every outreach message should follow this structure:

1. Relevant observation
2. Business problem
3. Diagnostic offer
4. Simple call to action

Example structure:

Observation:
Many companies discover hidden revenue once they analyze their product data.

Problem:
Fragmented product data often leads to poor search results and lost revenue.

Offer:
We run a short diagnostic to identify these issues.

CTA:
Would you be open to a short conversation next week?

---

# Outreach Type 1 — Data Revenue

Use when:
- messy product data
- large catalogs
- search or discovery problems

Buyer:
CTO
Digital Architect
Head of Data

Message angle:
Hidden revenue in product data.

Entry offer:
Data Revenue Diagnostic.

---

# Outreach Type 2 — AI Readiness

Use when:
- company exploring AI
- AI initiatives announced
- new AI leadership hired

Buyer:
CTO
CIO
Head of AI

Message angle:
AI ambitions blocked by data and architecture.

Entry offer:
AI Readiness Diagnostic.

---

# Outreach Type 3 — Commerce Optimization

Use when:
- ecommerce performance problems
- product discovery issues
- merchandising inefficiency

Buyer:
CMO
Ecommerce Director

Message angle:
Immediate revenue improvements.

Entry offer:
Commerce Optimization Pilot.

---

# Outreach Rule

Always:

- connect message to a real problem
- keep email under 120 words
- ask for a short conversation
- avoid selling large programs in first contact
