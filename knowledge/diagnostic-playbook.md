# Diagnostic Sales Playbook

Diagnostics are entry points into larger transformation work.

Never sell transformation first.

Always start with a clear business problem.

---

# Diagnostic Flow

1. Identify the business problem
2. Explain why it happens
3. Offer a diagnostic to analyze the problem
4. Deliver insights and roadmap
5. Expand into implementation

---

# Diagnostic Offers

## Data Revenue Diagnostic

Best for:

- retail
- large product catalogs
- messy product data

Goal:

Reveal hidden revenue opportunities.

---

## AI Readiness Diagnostic

Best for:

- companies exploring AI
- organizations with fragmented data

Goal:

Prepare architecture and data for AI deployment.
