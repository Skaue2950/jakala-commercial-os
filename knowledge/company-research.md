# Company Research Framework

Use this framework to analyze target companies before meetings or outreach.

The goal is to identify:

- strategic fit
- business problems
- best GTM strategy
- best entry offer
- likely buyer

---

# Company Overview

Analyze:

- company size
- industry
- digital maturity
- ecommerce or digital importance

Questions:

What is the company known for?
How important is digital to their business?

---

# Digital Ecosystem

Identify:

- ecommerce platforms
- CMS / DXP
- data architecture
- APIs and integrations

Signals to look for:

- fragmented systems
- legacy platforms
- slow digital delivery

---

# Data & AI Potential

Assess:

- product data complexity
- catalog size
- AI initiatives
- personalization capabilities

Questions:

Is the company exploring AI?
Is data structured and accessible?

---

# Strategic Fit

Evaluate using:

- icp-scoring.md
- deal-scoring.md
- strategy-mapping.md

Determine:

- ICP score
- deal score
- best GTM strategy

---

# Entry Wedge

Choose the most natural entry point:

1. Data Revenue Diagnostic
2. AI Readiness Diagnostic
3. Commerce Optimization Pilot
4. Executive Experience Strategy

Explain why this entry point works.

---

# Buyer Map

Identify likely stakeholders:

Technical buyers:
- CTO
- CIO
- Digital Architect

Commercial buyers:
- CMO
- Ecommerce Director

Strategic buyers:
- Transformation lead
- Digital experience lead

---

# Recommended Approach

Provide:

1. outreach angle
2. diagnostic offer
3. suggested meeting narrative
4. next commercial step
