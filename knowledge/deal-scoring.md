# Deal Scoring Model

This file helps prioritize accounts, signals, and pipeline opportunities.

Score each opportunity from 1 to 10 based on the criteria below.

## 1. Strategic Fit (1-2 points)

Does the company strongly match our GTM strategy and ICP?

High score if:
- retail / high-SKU commerce
- complex digital architecture
- fragmented data
- strong AI or transformation potential

## 2. Problem Urgency (1-2 points)

Is there a clear urgent business problem?

High score if:
- lost revenue from poor product discovery
- AI ambition blocked by bad data
- fragmented digital experience
- slow platform delivery

## 3. Entry Wedge Clarity (1-2 points)

Is there a clear entry offer we can sell now?

High score if:
- Data Revenue Diagnostic fits
- AI Readiness Diagnostic fits
- Commerce Optimization pilot fits
- Executive strategy engagement fits

## 4. Buyer Strength (1-2 points)

Do we know or can we identify the right buyer?

High score if:
- CTO / CIO / CMO / Ecommerce Director / Digital Architect is visible
- clear owner of the pain exists
- executive sponsor is likely

## 5. Expansion Potential (1-2 points)

Can this small entry point expand into a bigger transformation program?

High score if:
- data issue can expand into commerce optimization
- AI readiness can expand into architecture work
- pilot can expand into DXP / broader transformation

## Score Interpretation

9-10 = Highest priority  
7-8 = Strong opportunity  
5-6 = Worth monitoring  
1-4 = Low priority for now

## Scoring Rule

When reviewing a deal, always provide:

1. total score
2. reasoning by criterion
3. best-fit GTM strategy
4. best entry offer
5. recommended next action
