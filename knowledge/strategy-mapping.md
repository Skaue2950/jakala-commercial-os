# Strategy Mapping

This file helps map target accounts to the correct GTM entry strategy.

## Strategy 1 — Data Revenue Unlock

Use when:
- the company has a large product catalog
- product data is messy or fragmented
- search, discovery, or merchandising performance is weak
- the buyer is technical or architecture-led

Best personas:
- CTO
- Digital Architect
- Head of Product Data

Entry offer:
Data Revenue Diagnostic

Primary promise:
Unlock hidden revenue by fixing the data foundation.

Expansion path:
Commerce optimization → AI readiness → DXP transformation

---

## Strategy 2 — AI Readiness Accelerator

Use when:
- the company is actively talking about AI
- leadership wants AI use cases
- architecture or governance is not ready
- systems and APIs are fragmented

Best personas:
- CTO
- CIO
- Digital Architect
- Head of AI
- Transformation Lead

Entry offer:
AI Readiness Diagnostic

Primary promise:
Prepare data and architecture for real AI deployment.

Expansion path:
AI roadmap → architecture modernization → transformation program

---

## Strategy 3 — Commerce Optimization

Use when:
- the company is revenue-focused right now
- ecommerce performance is weak
- product discovery and search are underperforming
- the buyer is commercial or ecommerce-led

Best personas:
- CMO
- Ecommerce Director
- Digital Commerce Lead

Entry offer:
Commerce optimization pilot

Primary promise:
Increase digital sales through better product discovery and merchandising.

Expansion path:
Data orchestration → AI enablement → DXP transformation

---

## Strategy 4 — Experience Transformation Partner

Use when:
- the company already thinks strategically about platform change
- the digital ecosystem is fragmented across brands or markets
- governance and delivery are slow
- there is executive sponsorship for transformation

Best personas:
- CMO
- Digital Experience Architect
- CIO
- Enterprise Transformation Lead

Entry offer:
Executive digital experience strategy engagement

Primary promise:
Create a unified digital experience platform for long-term growth.

Expansion path:
Transformation roadmap → DXP program → AI-driven experience layer

---

## Mapping Rule

For each account, always determine:

1. Which GTM strategy fits best?
2. Why does it fit?
3. Which entry offer should we lead with?
4. What is the likely expansion path?
5. Who is the most relevant buyer?

If more than one strategy fits, choose:
- the most urgent business problem
- the easiest entry point
- the clearest commercial wedge
