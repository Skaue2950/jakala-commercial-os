## Overview

JAKALA sells through a diagnostic-led model: small entry engagements (diagnostics / pilots) that expand into larger transformation programs. Never lead with full transformation.

Three core practice areas:

1. **Commerce** — Shopify Practice + commerce optimization
2. **Data & AI** — Data revenue, AI readiness, Speedtrain orchestration
3. **Growth** — Hello Growth marketplace program for SaaS

## Offering 1 — Shopify Commerce Build

**Practice:** Commerce
**Type:** Core implementation
**Price range:** €80K–€500K
**Typical scope:** 3–12 months

### What we deliver
Full Shopify Plus commerce implementation. Includes UX/UI, platform setup, integrations, content migration, and go-live. Can include subscription (Recharge), loyalty (Growave), marketing automation (Klaviyo), customer service (Gorgias).

### Our credentials
- Shopify Premier Agency (one of 3 in Italy)
- 7+ years experience, 30+ certifications, 20+ projects last 2 years
- Clients: Kiton, La Prairie, Pomellato, Fratelli Rossetti, Alessandra Rich, Jacob Cohën, Barilla, Molteni

### Ideal for
- Retailers on Magento 2 / Adobe Commerce / WooCommerce / SFCC looking to replatform
- D2C brands wanting owned commerce
- Multi-market brands needing Shopify Plus across markets
- Fashion, luxury, FMCG, furniture, beauty

### Delivery methodology (6 phases)
1. Discovery / Check-up — current state assessment
2. Analysis / Design — business + technical + UX design
3. POC / SW Selection — risk-free validation + demo
4. Roadmap / Business case — timeline, budget, 3–5 year plan
5. Build / Delivery — agile development
6. Run / Scale — continuous support + growth

### Entry offer
**Shopify Check-up (Discovery)** — €15K–€30K
Current state assessment: platform audit, pain points, digital maturity, replatform readiness.

### Expansion path
Discovery → POC → Full build → Run/Scale → Data layer + personalization

### Do not sell to
- Companies with recent major platform investments (<2 years)
- Accounts deeply embedded in SAP Commerce or Salesforce Commerce with no migration signal
- B2B-only distributors without ecommerce ambition

## Offering 2 — Data Revenue Diagnostic + Speedtrain

**Practice:** Data & AI
**Type:** Diagnostic → platform
**Price range:** €50K–€150K (diagnostic) · €200K–€700K (Speedtrain implementation)
**Typical scope:** 6 weeks (diagnostic) · 3–9 months (implementation)

### What we deliver
**Diagnostic:** Audit of product data completeness, taxonomy quality, search relevance, PIM/ERP integration gaps, and AI data layer readiness. Output: quantified revenue opportunity + prioritised fix roadmap.

**Speedtrain:** JAKALA's proprietary data orchestration platform. Decouples intelligence (personalisation logic, product scoring, recommendations) from the frontend. Lives between PIM/ERP and the commerce/AI layer. Enables real-time personalisation at scale.

### Ideal for
- Retailers with large SKU counts (5,000+) and messy product data
- Multi-brand companies with fragmented catalogs
- Accounts with PIM in place but low adoption or poor output quality
- Companies running AI pilots that are failing due to data quality
- Loyalty programs with rich data but no personalisation activation

### Signals that indicate fit
- PIM recently implemented (Akeneo, Bluestone, Stibo, inRiver) — ready for next layer
- AI initiative announced but no data governance
- Low search conversion, high zero-results rate
- Multi-brand / multi-market with inconsistent catalog
- Loyalty program with 500K+ members but generic communication

### Entry offer
**Data Revenue Diagnostic** — €50K–€100K
Assess: product data completeness, search quality, AI data readiness, PIM output quality. Deliver: revenue hypothesis + prioritised fix roadmap.

### Expansion path
Diagnostic → Speedtrain implementation → AI personalisation → DXP transformation

**Practice:** Growth
**Type:** Flat-fee growth program
**Price range:** DKK 150,000–250,000 fixed fee (~€20K–€34K)
**Typical scope:** Full program delivery (4 phases)

### What we deliver
An integrated marketplace growth engine for SaaS scale-ups and Microsoft Solution Partners (SDCs). Combines marketplace acceleration (Azure Marketplace), demand generation, ABM, and continuous optimization.

**4 phases:**
1. **Insight Layer (Clarity Before Action):** Strategic Growth Blueprint, marketplace presence audit, testing-ready messaging variants
2. **Performance Platform (Activate with Precision):** High-converting site/landing environment, optimised marketplace presence, conversion-focused creative assets
3. **Growth Engine (Turn Data into Demand):** Live automated funnel, campaign assets, personalisation-ready site elements
4. **Intelligence Loop (Learn & Optimize Relentlessly):** Insight dashboard, continuous improvement plan, scale-up/tier expansion recommendations

### Ideal for
- SaaS scale-ups with Azure Marketplace presence
- Microsoft Solution Partners (SDCs, ISVs) needing pipeline
- Companies with product-market fit but low marketplace conversion
- SaaS companies with limited sales headcount needing automated pipeline

### Signals that indicate fit
- Listed on Azure Marketplace but low conversion
- Microsoft partnership (Gold/Solutions Partner) active
- SaaS product targeting enterprise customers
- Head of Sales/Growth role open — scaling commercial function

### Entry offer
**Marketplace Growth Diagnostic** — scoping session
Assess: marketplace listing quality, GTM messaging, competitive positioning, demand gen gaps. Output: Growth Blueprint + activation roadmap.

### Expansion path
Diagnostic → Insight Layer → Performance Platform → Growth Engine → Intelligence Loop

### Note
This offering targets a **different ICP** than the other four offerings. Requires a separate lead discovery track targeting Nordic SaaS companies and Microsoft SDC partners.
