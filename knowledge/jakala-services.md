# JAKALA Services — Full Portfolio Reference

Last updated: 2026-03-08

This file is the reference for pitch-partner matching. It covers ALL JAKALA services — global portfolio + Nordic-specific offerings.

## How to use this file

When matching services to a lead, always prioritize:
1. The account's confirmed signals (what they have done or announced)
2. The account's scale and data complexity
3. The buyer persona in the room (CTO, CMO, CDO, CEO)
4. The entry offer — always the smallest credible first step
5. The expansion path — what comes after the entry

### 1. Commerce Experience (B2C + B2B)

**Practice:** Technology — Commerce & Enabling Solutions
**Nordic entry offer:** Commerce Optimization Pilot (€40–80K · 8 weeks) or Shopify Commerce Build (€80–500K)
**Full programme:** €200K–€3M+

**What we deliver:**
Scalable ecommerce experiences for B2C and B2B channels. Covers platform implementation (Shopify Plus, commercetools, Salesforce Commerce Cloud), UX/UI, checkout optimization, B2B catalog setup, product discovery, and conversion rate improvement.

**Best fit for:**
- Retailers with underperforming ecommerce (low conversion, poor search, bad B2B experience)
- Brands replatforming from Magento 2 / Adobe Commerce / WooCommerce
- Companies launching or scaling B2B commerce
- Multi-brand retailers needing unified commerce architecture

**Signals that indicate fit:**
- B2B commerce launched but conversion is low
- Platform over 4 years old (Magento, EPiServer, SAP)
- Distribution center automation investment — digital catalog must keep pace
- Acquisition adding new brands/markets to existing platform

**Buyer persona:** Head of Ecommerce · Digital Director · CTO · CMO
**Do not sell to:** Companies with <2 year-old major platform investment and no migration signal

### 2. Product Experience — PIM / DAM / Catalog

**Practice:** Technology — Commerce & Enabling Solutions
**Nordic entry offer:** Data Revenue Diagnostic (€50–100K · 6–10 weeks)
**Full programme:** Speedtrain implementation (€200K–€700K)

**What we deliver:**
Product Information Management (PIM), Digital Asset Management (DAM), and sales channel management. Ensures consistent, high-quality product catalogues distributed across all channels. Includes JAKALA's proprietary Speedtrain data orchestration platform.

**Speedtrain:** JAKALA's proprietary platform that decouples intelligence (personalisation, product scoring, recommendations) from the frontend. Lives between PIM/ERP and the commerce/AI layer.

**Best fit for:**
- Retailers with 5,000+ SKUs and messy product data
- Multi-brand companies with fragmented catalogs
- Companies where PIM is in place but output quality is poor
- Businesses running AI tools that fail due to data quality
- Loyalty programs with data but no personalisation activation

**Signals that indicate fit:**
- PIM recently implemented (Akeneo, Bluestone, Stibo, inRiver) — ready for next layer
- Low search conversion, high zero-results rate
- Multi-brand / multi-market with inconsistent catalog
- AI initiative announced but no data governance
- Loyalty program with 500K+ members but generic communication

**Buyer persona:** CTO · Head of Data · CDO · Head of Product
**Key differentiator:** Speedtrain is proprietary — no competitor offers this exact layer
